The storm was raging out at sea, waves crashing violently.
The lighthouse stood out against the dark sky, its light piercing through the rain.
She leaned out of the window, watching the chaos unfold.
"Stay inside! Don't go out in this weather," her father warned.
A loud clap of thunder drowned out his words.
She pulled out her phone to capture the moment.
The power went out suddenly, leaving the house in darkness.
Her brother stumbled out of his room, holding a flashlight.
"Looks like we'll have to wait it out," he said, yawning.
The storm finally passed, leaving a calm, eerie silence.
